I have many hobbies & interests and am always seeking to learn new things... I consider life boring if I'm not learning something.

  • Photography.
    • Much of what I photograph is wildlife.
  • Naturalist.

  • Walking.
    • My walking is often paired with my photography and my Naturalist skills. Sometimes even if I want to just chill and slow down, I can't help myself and randomly record the species I come across... What can I say, I love animals, even those that bite me and am allergic to (looking at you Mosquitos).

    • I find that writing poetry is therapeutic, it allows me to vent off when I become overwhelmed.

  • Cooking & Baking.
    • I was taught from a young age, primary school, to cook. I remember making Chapatis and yoghurt mint sauce from an Indian woman, I remember her tasting all our results and considered mine the best one.
