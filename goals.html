Beginners Guide to Terrestrial Invertebrates:
Field Studies Council: July '22


  • Invertebrate Taxonomy & Morphology.
  • The Insect Orders.

Guess what, some of this was self taught too. I took this course to better aid my identification skills and I now have much greater confidence in identifying specific animal groups. Some insect groups look similar to one another, but they have small features which distinguish them.
Beetles and Shieldbugs have similar charactaristics, particularly that of having a hard carapace. Because of that, people who are unfamiliar with the difference would typically call Shieldbugs beetles.

I am an amateur naturalist and am passionate about wildlife. It was Sir David Attenborough who inspired me to become one. Starting with birds.
I do occasionally send data to various wildlife groups, such as British Dragonfly Society, Hoverfly Recording Scheme and British Trust for Ornithology which I am credited for my data along with other volunteers in their publication BTO Bird Atlas 2007-11.
